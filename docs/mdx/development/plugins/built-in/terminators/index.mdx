---
title: Definition
description: Plugins that handle the final processing of a connection or request.
icon: TextWrap
---

Terminators are the final destination of a flow. They implement the `Terminator` or `L7Terminator` trait and are responsible for:

- **Proxying**: Forwarding raw TCP/UDP connections.
- **Upgrading**: Signaling a protocol transition (e.g., TCP -> TLS).
- **Responding**: Generating an immediate HTTP response.
