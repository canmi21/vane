# Code of Conduct

We are developers. We do not need a wall of rules.

1. **Do not do things you believe are wrong.**
2. **Until a contributor becomes a long-term maintainer, the maintainer has the right to make the final call.**
3. **Read more at [Contributor Covenant 3.0 Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/3/0/code_of_conduct)**

That's it.
